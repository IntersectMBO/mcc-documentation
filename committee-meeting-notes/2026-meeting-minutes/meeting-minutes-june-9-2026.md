Decisions/Actions
-----------------

#### Decisions

* Official Communication Channel: Discord was strictly reaffirmed as the primary and only official channel for all formal committee tracking, votes, and binding decisions, while Telegram is strictly relegated to informal notifications and reminders.
* Bi-Weekly X Spaces: The committee decided to maintain the bi-weekly Monday cadence for the community X Spaces (Open Office Hours) to continuously gather ecosystem feedback.

#### Actions

* Workstream Assignments: All elected committee members are required to log their specific Q3 workstream commitments and designated sticky note colors in the internal Discord channel to build a structured layout in ClickUp.
* Enterprise Plus Framework: Anuj, Lorenzo, and Abhik will meet this upcoming Thursday to outline the definitions, persona templates, and elevated fee structures for the new Enterprise Plus tier.
* Asynchronous Document Reviews: Committee members must access the shared drive links to review and leave comments on the Member Participation Leveling and Membership Offerings drafts prior to the next bi-weekly session.
* SME Framework Finalization: The committee will coordinate and solidify the onboarding framework to transition active observers and volunteers (such as Wilco and Chaya) into official Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) during the next meeting.
* Intersect Insight Speakers: Abhik to coordinate the lineup of new committee volunteers willing to speak about their election experiences on the upcoming live broadcast.
* Leadership Alignment: Martin and Sanjaya will interface with the Intersect Steering Committee (ISC) to align the committee's newly established Q3 priority boxes with Intersect's macro strategy.
